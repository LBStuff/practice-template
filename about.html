About

I consider myself a visual novel and adventure game developer. I'm based in California. Most of the games I develop follow the stories of women who find themselves in substantial circumstances.

I draw inspiration from novels such as Glen Cook's The Black Company series, J. R. R. Tolkien's The Lord of the Rings, and H.P. Lovecraft's various works.

You can find me on Mastodon, but I have yet to post.
