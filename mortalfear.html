Mortal Fear

A Sierra-style point-and-click adventure game.

Mortal Fear is the temporary title of a Sierra-style point-and-click adventure game that I am currently developing in Adventure Game Studio.

You play as Caren Blackwood, a young woman employed alongside her husband William Blackwood by Edmund Sauer. One afternoon, you find yourself awake in your bed, with your husband amiss. In fact, there is no one in the Sauer mansion at all.
