Inheritance

A Visual Novel by Thief

Inheritance is a visual novel in which you play as Veleria Garris, a mercenary-for-hire who is betrayed by her employer. After nearly escaping with the information you were hired to find, you are killed by Vos'iin Ceras'Beur, a hired hand of the sorceress Alk'Hath.

You are found by Adrian Giarmund and nursed back to health, although how you survived is still a mystery to you.
