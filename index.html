Inheritance

A Visual Novel by Thief

Inheritance is a visual novel in which you play as Veleria Garris, a mercenary-for-hire who is betrayed by her employer. After nearly escaping with the information you were hired to find, you are killed by Vos'iin Ceras'Beur, a hired hand of the sorceress Alk'Hath.

You are found by Adrian Giarmund and nursed back to health, although how you survived is still a mystery to you.

Untitled

An Adventure Game by Thief

Untitled is an adventure game in which you play as Caren Blackwood, a young engineer trapped in a nightmarish situation in which you must find your husband and escape.

About

Thief is a visual novel and adventure game developer based in California. Most of the games they develop follow the stories of women who find themselves in substantial circumstances.

They draw inspiration from novels such as Glen Cook's The Black Company series, J. R. R. Tolkien's The Lord of the Rings, and H.P. Lovecraft's various works.
